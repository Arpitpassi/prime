About Us

PRIME (Promoting Research in Mathematics and Engineering) is a student-driven community for anyone curious about mathematics and its real-world impact.


Through interactive workshops and coding sessions, PRIME makes learning mathematics practical, engaging, and hands-on.

Events

P.T.S.D

P.T.S.D (PRIME's Thrilling Session of Discussions) is our flagship weekly event where mathematical curiosity meets collaborative problem-solving.


Join us every week to sharpen your problem-solving skills and be part of a community that celebrates the beauty of mathematical thinking.

Path of PRIME

Path of PRIME is your official gateway into our community. Designed as a roadmap for aspiring members.


Take your first step on the Path to PRIME and discover where mathematics can take you.

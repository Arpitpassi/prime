About Us

PRIME (Promoting Research in Mathematics and Engineering) is a student-driven community for anyone curious about mathematics and its real-world impact. We provide a space to explore ideas, sharpen problem-solving skills, and connect mathematics with engineering, research, and industry.


Through interactive workshops, coding sessions, talks, competitions, and collaborative study groups, PRIME makes learning mathematics practical, engaging, and hands-on. Our goal is to foster curiosity, collaboration, and innovation, empowering students to grow academically and apply mathematical thinking to real-world challenges.

Events

P.T.S.D

P.T.S.D (PRIME's Thrilling Session of Discussions) is our flagship weekly event where mathematical curiosity meets collaborative problem-solving. Every session brings together students passionate about mathematics to tackle challenging problems, explore new concepts, and share innovative approaches.


Through interactive discussions, brain-teasing puzzles, and peer-led explorations, P.T.S.D creates an engaging environment where learning happens naturally. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced problem solver, these sessions are designed to challenge your thinking, expand your mathematical toolkit, and connect you with like-minded peers who share your passion for mathematics.


Join us every week to sharpen your problem-solving skills, discover new mathematical techniques, and be part of a community that celebrates the beauty of mathematical thinking.
